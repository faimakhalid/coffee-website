About Coffee

Coffee Wagera (CW) was launched in April 2018, with a dream to become the biggest and the best coffee brand of Pakistan! From the beginning, there’s a clear focus on social responsibility which includes staff welfare, low-waste practices, supporting women’s empowerment and inclusivity.

Pakistan’s largest coffee brand with 13 locations in 4 cities, in less than 7 years!

Dine-in locations also offer free workspace, free wifi, free drinking water!

Excellent coffees with freshly roasted beans from local roaster.

Low prices with special discounts for students and senior citizens.

We support women entrepreneurs, low-waste practices and inclusion.

We have a loyalty app, online store and barista academy too!

Our Founder & Chief Happiness Officer

Mush Panjwani has been a sales, marketing, and training expert for over 35 years. He has worked with corporates, leaders, and entrepreneurs in 20 countries. Mush is also the author of Dhinchak Life, a book about living a fantastic life!

Mush launched Coffee Wagera on 1st April 2018 with a dream of making it the best and the biggest coffee brand across Pakistan.

Mush is also a certified ‘happiness trainer’ and trains at corporates and non-profits. More about Mush at www.MushPanjwani.com
